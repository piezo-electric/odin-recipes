Lasagna

A tasty pile of lasagna!

This recipe is simply amazing and adds a delectable twist to the standard italian fare. This Lasagna recipe is sure to be a hit for an office pot luck or even a family reunion (don't tell Nona!). I made sure to select for ingredients and cooking styles that would allow for a cheap, but delicious meal for your family and friends.

Arguably, I'm certain you can feed an entire village worth of people with this recipe. Anyways, you'll need the following ingredients and tools to properly make this dish! Don't worry, it's not that much of an ordeal. You likely got everything already!

Ingredients

  • One 16 oz package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
