Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons miso paste (white or red, depending on your preference )
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sake (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (shiitake, oyster, or your choice)
  • 1 cup baby spinach or bok choy
  • 1 cup sliced green onions
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved (optional)
  • Nori sheets, sliced into thin strips (for garnish)

Preparation steps

  1. Prepare the Broth: In a large pot, heat the sesame oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, sauté for a minute until fragrant. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring it to a simmer.
  2. Dissolve Miso Paste: In a small bowl, mix the miso paste with a ladle of hot broth to dissolve it completely. Add the dissolved miso paste back to the pot and stir well.
  3. Season the Broth: Add soy sauce and sake (if using) to the broth. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste.
    4. Prepare the Toppings: Boil the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a separate pan, sauté the sliced mushrooms until they are tender. Blanch the baby spinach or bok choy in the broth for a minute until wilted.
  4. Assemble the Ramen: Divide the cooked noodles among serving bowls.Pour the hot miso broth over the noodles. Arrange the sautéed mushrooms, blanched greens, sliced green onions, and boiled eggs on top. Garnish with sliced nori.
  5. Serve: Serve the miso ramen hot and enjoy!
