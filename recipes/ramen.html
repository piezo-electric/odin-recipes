Ramen

An awesome bowl of ramen

Ramen can hit the spot whether it's hot or cold! This recipe is for hot ramen! Specifically, Tonkatsu ramen. This will put a pep in your step (hah,get it?PEPPERS!) due to the shishito peppers added to the broth.

This recipe can serve 3-4 people and prep time requires at least 14 hrs for broth to cooking.
